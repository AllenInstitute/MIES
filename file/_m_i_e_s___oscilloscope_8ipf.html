Parameters

  • panelTitle: panel title

  • dataAcqOrTP: One of DATA_ACQUISITION_MODE or TEST_PULSE_MODE

  • fifoPos: Position of the hardware DAQ fifo to keep track of the position which will be written next

  • deviceID: device ID
